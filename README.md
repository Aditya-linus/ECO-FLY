# ECO-FLY

### Environmental & Agricultural Drone Platform

## What will be ECO-FLY?


ECO-FLY will be a modular FPV drone platform designed for agricultural monitoring and environmental observation. and in the future maybe work autonomously.

The drone will combine a lightweight carbon fiber airframe, brushless propulsion motors, a real-time FPV video transmission sutaible for work in large field areas.
---
## Why I Built It

Agricultural monitoring and environmental observation often require large areas to be inspected manually. or the current monitoring systems or existing drone tech is not affordable to everyone and requires professional training.

The goal of ECO-FLY is to develop a low-cost aerial vision system which can help in surveillance of fields and will be easy to operate without any professional training. ECO-FLY aims to provide farmers with a low cost monitoring system more efficient that existing solutions and help them produce the maximum yeild.

The project also serves as a learning platform for me as I have a very keen intrest in drone engineering, flight control systems, wireless communication, and aerial robotics and I have previously also worked on drones so this will act as a way to enhance my skills and hopefully solve an existing problem.

## System Overview

The platform consists of:

- Carbon fiber frame
- Brushless motors
- Electronic Speed Controller (ESC)
- Flight Controller
- FPV camera
- Video transmitter (VTX)
- ELRS communication system
- LiPo power system


Control Flow:

Radio Controller
↓
ELRS Receiver
↓
Flight Controller
↓
ESC
↓
Motors
↓
Propellers

Video Flow:

FPV Camera
↓
Video Transmitter
↓
FPV Goggles
