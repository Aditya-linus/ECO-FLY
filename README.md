# ECO-FLY

### Environmental & Agricultural Drone Platform

## Overview

ECO-FLY is a modular FPV drone platform designed for agricultural monitoring, environmental observation, and future autonomous aerial applications.

The project focuses on creating a reliable, repairable, and expandable drone system that can serve as a foundation for research, education, and field deployment.

---

## Objectives

- Develop a modular FPV drone platform
- Enable agricultural and environmental monitoring
- Document design and testing procedures
- Publish development progress openly

---

## Applications

- Crop monitoring
- Field surveying
- Environmental observation
- Remote inspection
- Educational robotics
- Future autonomous flight research
